After anguished moral struggle and much impassioned argument before the High Court, Judge Natalie Jeremijenko has settled matters and given us a winner! The victorious candidate is:

"The New World Order Firing Squad

"An automated firing squad confronts a large, bounding balloon in an installation shooting gallery. The guns dramatically respond to the keywords 'Ready,' 'Aim,' and 'Fire.' On 'ready' the guns chamber a round, on 'aim' they unsteadily converge at the target, and at 'fire' they shoot pellets. But the cybernetic guns cannot be directly touched, are unbearably quirky, and are frequently disobedient. Furthermore, the balloon itself is surprisingly tough and slippery, bounding around at random directions and speeds. The intent of this installation is to increasingly excite and frustrate an ever-growing and ever-more-bloodthirsty crowd, demanding the balloon's annihilation. The frantic voices are recorded and played back after the balloon finally meets its end."


Accepting the crown for his heartbreaking work of staggering genius is: bruces@well.com (Bruce Sterling). Bruce has graciously agreed to give us a few words about his insightful work of technosocial criticism.

Bruce Sterling (sobbing unevenly, clutching tiara to his head and shifting unwieldy bouquet):

So I just want to thank Supreme Judge Natalie, and my extensive family, especially my brother the Governor, and that attractive Attorney General, and the majority in the Florida House, and the minority on the Florida Supreme Court, and Fox News cable, and, most especially, the designer of the Vote-o-Matic. Thank goodness that our long ordeal is over. Now everyone in our Viridian feudal theocracy can once again unite behind the Pope-Emperor."
